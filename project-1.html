Empowering Women's Ministry.

26 November 2018Present

The Fix Ministry.

In partnership with a nonprofit women's ministry dedicated to connecting, encouraging, and celebrating women, I developed a comprehensive digital strategy to enhance their outreach and engagement efforts. This initiative aimed to boost their visibility, increase event participation, and foster a supportive community among women.

Nati really saw our business and understood what we were trying to accomplish. She made our ideas better and made them real for us so that we could share it.

The Approach.

To achieve these goals, I began by conducting a thorough assessment of their current digital presence and identifying areas for improvement. Key strategies included:

  • Website Revamp: Redesigned the ministry's website to create a more user-friendly interface and enhance the visitor experience. This involved streamlining navigation, incorporating engaging visuals, and ensuring mobile responsiveness.
  • Content Strategy: Developed a content calendar that included blog posts, newsletters, and social media updates to keep the audience informed and engaged. The content focused on empowering stories, upcoming events, and resources for personal growth.
  • Social Media Campaign: Launched a targeted social media campaign to increase followers and engagement. This involved creating shareable content, hosting virtual events, and collaborating with influencers in the community.

The Impact.

As a result of these efforts, the ministry experienced a 40% increase in website traffic and a 30% rise in event registrations. The enhanced digital presence helped them connect with more women, strengthening their community and mission.
