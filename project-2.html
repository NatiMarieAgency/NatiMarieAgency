Teen Outreach Program Development and Branding.

8 January 20236 May 2023

ChangeMakers Youth Network.

Working with a teen outreach program focused on empowering at-risk youth, my goal was to enhance their online presence and engagement with teens and their families. The program aimed to provide educational resources, mentoring, and support to help teens reach their full potential in business and in life.

She literally walked us through what it takes to build out our company and the branding.

The Approach.

To support this initiative, I implemented the following strategies:

  • Brand Identity: Developed a cohesive brand identity that resonated with teens and reflected the program's values. This included designing a modern logo, color palette, and typography that appealed to a younger audience.
  • Interactive Website Features: Introduced interactive elements on the website, such as forums, chat support, and downloadable resources, to facilitate engagement and provide a platform for teens to connect with mentors and peers.
  • Audience Outreach: Executed a digital outreach strategy that included influencer and affiliate marketing, email campaigns, and partnerships with local schools and community organizations to reach a broader audience.

The Impact.

These initiatives resulted in a 50% increase in website engagement and a significant boost in program enrollment. The program's enhanced digital presence allowed them to reach more teens and provide vital support and resources.
